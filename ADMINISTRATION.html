HEAD MASTERS MESSAGE


Shilo Nile Star nursery and primary school, found in Njeru Municipality.
Buikwe District along Bugerere Road, 0.5 km from Nile Stage, contains well trained.
selected and dedicated staff that are ready to transform a learner to the parents expectations.
Enough classrooms which can accommodate learners
well spacious dormitories and motherly matrons who narrow the gap between home and school are all present

Excellent performance is realized every year, producing a good number of aggregate 4 candidates.

Our learners are fed of a balanced diet which has a positive impact on their performance but alongside teaching,
teachers train learners in Games and Sports, Music Dance and Drama and Swimming.


A chain of command is followed from top to bottom that helps in the smooth running of all functions and operations within and out of the school causing quality service.
